Fascination Meets Innovation

The smartphones are sold online initially. During 2020-2021, the iQOO introduced six smartphones in the Chinese market and three smartphones in the Indian market, as of May 2021. iQOO is a Chinese consumer electronics manufacturer that is a subsidiary of Vivo. The company was founded in 2019 and began operating independently in February of that year. iQOO is known for developing phones that are designed for gaming, with the first phone being named the official gaming phone of the King Pro League. The company's name stands for "I Quest On and On".
